Privacy Policy

Last update: 06/08/2023

Your security is important to us. For this reason, any personal data you share with us is treated with utmost sensitivity and protected.

As Chat Koala, the data controller, with this privacy and personal data protection policy, we aim to inform you about which personal data of yours will be processed for what purpose, with whom and why the processed data may be shared, our data processing methods and legal reasons, and what rights you have regarding the processed data.

Collected Personal Data, Collection Method, and Legal Basis

Your identity (such as name, surname, date of birth), contact (address, email address, phone, IP, location), employment, social media, financial information, as well as your visual and auditory records are collected by us through technologies like cookies, and sometimes obtained from third parties such as analytic providers, advertising networks, search information providers, technology providers, through automated or non-automated methods, and will be recorded, stored, and updated within the scope and duration of our service and contractual relationship, based on the legitimate interest processing condition.

Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data

The personal data you share with us will be processed for the purpose of establishing contracts with you in order for you to benefit from our services, fulfilling the requirements of the services we provide in the best way and in accordance with our contracts, ensuring the use of your rights arising from these contracts, developing our products and services according to your needs and informing you about these developments, and also to bring you together with more comprehensive service providers within the legal frameworks and to fulfill legal obligations (sharing personal data with judicial and administrative authorities upon request), during the contract and service period, in a manner that is appropriate and proportional to the purpose and will be updated accordingly.

Possible Recipients and Purposes of Transferring Collected Personal Data

The personal data you share with us may be transferred, subject to necessary technical and administrative measures, to domestic and international third parties, institutions, and organizations with whom we have contractual relationships, collaborations, and partnerships, and to judicial and administrative authorities upon request, for the purpose of conducting our activities.

Your Rights as the Data Subject Whose Personal Data is Processed

According to Article 11 of the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), everyone can exercise the following rights by applying to the data controller:

  1. Learn whether personal data is processed,
  2. Request information if personal data has been processed,
  3. Learn the purpose of processing personal data and whether they are used appropriately for that purpose,
  4. Be informed about third parties to whom personal data is transferred, whether domestically or internationally,
  5. Request correction of personal data if it is incomplete or incorrect,
  6. Request deletion or destruction of personal data,
  7. Request notification of operations carried out pursuant to subparagraphs (e) and (f) to third parties to whom personal data have been transferred,
  8. Object to the occurrence of a result against the data subject arising from the analysis of personal data solely through automated systems,
  9. Request compensation for damages if the data subject incurs damages due to the unlawful processing of personal data.

You can contact us via info@chatkoala.app to exercise the rights mentioned above.
